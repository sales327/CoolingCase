FAQ

Straight answers for an early concept.

What is the current state?

Prototyping.

Will there be iPad support?

Yes, planned.

Which devices will it support?

Major phones and tablets.

What technology will be used?

Current development focuses on fan cooling with directed airflow. Depending on the use case, vented concepts or water-evaporative cooling for extreme scenarios may also be explored.

Is this available now?

Not yet. Under development, planned release end of 2026.
